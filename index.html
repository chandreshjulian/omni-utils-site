OmniUtils

The developer toolbelt for your browser

OmniUtils lives in your toolbar and gives you instant access to a JSON formatter with syntax highlighting and collapse/expand, a clipboard manager that captures everything you copy across the web, and a codec tool for Base64 and JWT encoding/decoding — all without leaving the browser.

json-formatter

JSON Formatter

Paste any JSON — including stringified or double-encoded — and get instant syntax-highlighted output with collapsible nodes and line numbers.

clipboard-manager

Clipboard Manager

Automatically captures text you copy on any web page. Access your full copy history instantly from the popup.

codec

Codec

Encode and decode Base64 strings and JWT tokens. Decode JWTs without a secret; verify signatures when you have one.

settings

Settings

Choose your popup size and storage mode. Four size presets from compact to wide — configure it to fit your workflow.
